About the Society

The Rachleff Scholars Society, founded in 2011, is a group dedicated to fostering community within the Rachleff Scholars Program. Our goals are to improve the camaraderie within the RSS community and provide guidance and support to underclassmen Rachleff Scholars. The activities hosted by the organization range from academic guest speakers discussing their research to social events such as baseball games and barbecues.
